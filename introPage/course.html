Course Selection System

This is designed for helping Zhejiang University students better arrange their courses.

Firstly, they can log in with their student ID and password. We will help to synchronize with their timetable for further planning.

After entering, they are offered with the list D of all the courses available.

They can choose different modes by click A.

When they click B we can rank the courses by historical GPA or teacher ratings, which can greatly satisfy the need of students.

C is a search bar. They can search by the course name, teacher name, time, location...

After adding the wanted courses, as shown in E, they can press F to trigger the automated course arranger.

All the wanted courses are listed in D .

There are three buttons of B can be clicked, according to three different modes, namely highest expected GPA, highest teacher ratings or highest scores.

E presents the timetable automated generated. And C displays relative information.

Three different candidates C are for each mode.

More functions are under developing for better handling the conflicts of different courses.
