4-digit Gaussian Elimination

It's a little demo for my Numerical Analysis class. I'm going to solve the linear system by applying Gaussian elimination using 4-digit arithmetic with rounding.

However, a huge error happens.

By theoritical analysis (It is left out here:)), we find the trouble maker and fix the problem by switching the row or the column of the linear system.

This little demo is going to show the big improvement on the naive solution.

You can also see more general cases by manually changing the coefficient.
