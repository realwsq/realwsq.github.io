Heap Visualization

This is Heap Visualization with the background of Shortest Path Algorithm.

It's a little demo for my Advanced Data Structures class. I think this Visualization vividly display how Fibonacci Heap, Binomial Heap and Binary Heap grows.

First of all, a force directed graph presents us with a graph of different nodes and links. We want to find out the shortest path between two arbitrary nodes with Dijkastra Algorithm. We can text the name of the node to the input, like "Tholomyes" and "Fantine" and then press "Go". (The "animation speed"" is used for adjusting the speed of animation.)

Then our visualization goes as expected. :)
